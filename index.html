ABOUT MUMANU

MUMANU Polyester Industries Ltd is one of the Biggest & Pioneer of PET Recycling Company in Bangladesh with a special focus on serving superior value to the environment, society, employees & customers.

WHO WE ARE?

Founded by Mr. Abul Kalam Mohammad Musa (Managing Director) on December 15, 2014, on about 600 decimal of land with a 1,38,200 sft operational area,we are producing 120 tonsbof PSF fiber per day in multiple lines.


MUMANU Polyester Industries Ltd. is a recycled Polyester Staple Fiber (PSF) producing company located in Panishail, Singair, Manikganj.


Starting its wheels in September 2017, it is the most professional and technically advanced polyester fiber plant in Bangladesh.


The main product of this company is high-quality regular polyerter staple fiber, made from recycled PET flakes. With a capacity of producing about 25600 tons annually along with a scope for expanding further.
